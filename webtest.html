America

New York

New York City, often called "The Big Apple," is the largest city in the United States. It is famous for its iconic landmarks like Times Square, Central Park, the Statue of Liberty, and the Empire State Building. A global hub for finance, culture, and entertainment, New York offers a diverse and vibrant lifestyle.

Italy

Rome

Rome, the capital of Italy, is known as the "Eternal City" for its 2,500-year history. It is home to famous landmarks like the Colosseum, the Pantheon, and Vatican City. Rome combines ancient ruins with modern culture, offering art, history, and world-renowned Italian cuisine.

Thailand

Bangkok

Bangkok, the capital city of Thailand, is known for its vibrant street life, ornate temples, and bustling markets. Iconic landmarks include the Grand Palace, Wat Arun, and Wat Phra Kaew. The city blends modern skyscrapers with traditional culture, offering a unique experience to visitors.

France

Paris

Paris, the capital of France, is known for its rich history, art, and culture. Famous landmarks include the Eiffel Tower, the Louvre Museum, and Notre-Dame Cathedral. The city is also renowned for its fashion, cuisine, and romantic ambiance, making it a popular destination for tourists worldwide.

India

New Delhi

New Delhi is the capital of India, located in the northern part of the country. It was established in 1931 and serves as the center of India's government. The city is famous for landmarks like India Gate, Rashtrapati Bhavan, and Qutub Minar, blending history with modern development.

Brazil

Brasilia

Brazil is the largest country in South America, known for its vast rainforests, vibrant culture, and famous landmarks like the Amazon River and Christ the Redeemer statue. Its capital is Brasília, and the official language is Portuguese. Brazil is renowned for its Carnival festival, samba music, and as a powerhouse in football (soccer).

Lisbon

Lisbon is the capital and largest city of Portugal, located along the Tagus River near the Atlantic Ocean. Known for its hilly landscape, historic neighborhoods, and vibrant culture, it features landmarks like Belém Tower, Jerónimos Monastery, and São Jorge Castle.

Turkey

Ankara

Ankara is the capital of Turkey and the country’s second-largest city after Istanbul. Located in the central part of Anatolia, it serves as the political and administrative center of Turkey. The city is known for landmarks like Anıtkabir, the mausoleum of Mustafa Kemal Atatürk, and its rich history, blending ancient and modern influences.
